WiFinder

Explore

Users can choose any combination of time, date and room and, from the data that we have stored in our database, interesting statistics can be generated. New data can be uploaded at any time in the data upload section of our website.

To display the statistics we have used a program called Bokeh which allowed us to present the data in interesting ways.

Estimator

Users can choose a specific time, date and room and, from the data that we have stored in our database, our models will generate the estimated occupancy of that room using the wifi log data. New data can be uploaded at any time in the data upload section of our website.

After extensive data analysis, we have chosen the three best models which give the most accurate predictions. Our binary model predicts whether a class is empty or occupied. Our linear model predicts the actual headcount of the class. Our last model categorises occupancy into high, medium or low and predicts occupancy of a class in these bins.

Data input

New data can be uploaded at any time in the data upload section of our website. This helps with updating our models to provide insight into room occupnacy levels over a period of time. This insight can be used to better inform future room allocation and timetables
